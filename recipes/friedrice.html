Fried Rice

The national treasure of Asian-Americans.

The fried rice dish is a simple yet elegant meal.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Eggs
  • Spam
  • Green Onions
  • Anything else you want to add

Instructions

  1. Preheat skillet to medium-high heat before adding sesame oil
  2. Add any random foods you want and fry until tender
  3. Set cooked foods aside and scramble beaten eggs
  4. Combine spam, rice, and cooked foods and mix
  5. Pour soy sauce and mix until sauce is heated thouroughly
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve
